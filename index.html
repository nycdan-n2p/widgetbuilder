Easy Customization Guide

To customize your widget, simply modify the aiWidgetConfig object at the top of this HTML file:

  • Colors: Change primaryColor and secondaryColor
  • Position: Set position to 'bottom-right', 'bottom-left', 'top-right', or 'top-left'
  • Size: Adjust width, height, and iconSize
  • Content: Customize greeting, placeholder, and title
  • API: Add your apiToken and apiUrl
